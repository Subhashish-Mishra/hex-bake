How It Works

Standard alpha compositing (Porter-Duff "over" operator) applied per channel.

Cfinal = Coverlay × α + Cbackground × (1 − α)
Overlay color channel (R, G, or B)
Background color channel (R, G, or B)
α — opacity value (0.0 → 1.0)

Choose Your Colors

Set Opacity
